The Idea

Commitment, made effortless.

Scrolling already feels wonderful — those little moments of joy, on tap. TokLock just makes sure nothing pulls you away too soon. Real presence takes time, so we help you stay a while.

Every session becomes a gentle promise to yourself: the next 20 minutes are yours to enjoy.

Old screen-time apps take your time away.

A "limit reached" popup, nagging you off the very thing that makes you happy. That's not care — it's a killjoy.

TokLock gives your time back.

Open Instagram or TikTok and we lovingly hold the space — so you stay fully present until your goal is reached. No more quitting early.

How it works

Four steps to a fuller day.

STEP 01

Settle in

Open your favorite app — or start a session on purpose. The clock starts. You're in the zone.

STEP 02

Stay present

A cozy minimum session runs (say 45 min) toward your daily goal (say 8 hrs) you build up across the day.

STEP 03

Drifting off?

Reach for the home button and a friendly nudge — building warmly — guides you right back to what you love.

STEP 04

Celebrate

Hit your goal and you've earned the rest of the evening off — until midnight. Then reset. New day, new goal.
